Privacy Policy for NextGoal Running

Effective Date: 2026-01-18

Overview

NextGoal Running collects only what’s necessary to operate the app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • App data stored locally on your device (e.g., race info via Hive). This data is not uploaded.
  • Optional diagnostics/crash data provided by the OS or platform services.
  • Feedback emails you send to jemcodev@gmail.com.

How We Use Information

  • Provide features like race tools, calculators, strategies, and checklists.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and fix bugs.
  • Respond to support requests.

Permissions

  • Storage: Save local app data (Hive).
  • Network: Retrieve race information from third-party sources you request.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers solely to operate app features.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Security

We use platform best practices. Sensitive personal data is not transmitted off-device without your action.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can delete local app data in-app or by uninstalling.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of support emails you’ve sent.

Contact

Email: jemcodev@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We’ll revise the Effective Date above when changes occur.
